- banners or pop-up bars that show discounts, message, warnings etc are also known as hello bars
- its not preferred to serve them from database
- yes, the information contained in those hello bars is stored in database, but they are served from the redis layer
- those hello bars are shown to every user, so it doesn't make sense to server them from database, better serve them from redis
- hello bars can be made static too (no issues with that)
- usually the information regarding those hello bars is updated through database panel
