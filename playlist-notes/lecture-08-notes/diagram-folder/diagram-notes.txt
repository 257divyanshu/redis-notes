- publisher can be an app or a service or a worker

- redis is like an intermediatory between publisher and subscriber
- in redis, we need to make channel(s)

- there can be many subscribers

- types of subscribers:
  - active
  - inactive

- the publisher publishes stuff on channels
- the stuff's configuration contains:
  - the name of the channel on which the stuff needs to be published
  - message to be published

- so from our end, we only need to publish stuff on channels

- subscribers actively listen to the channels
- subscribers receive something called "delivery updates" from channels
- inactive subscribers do not get the updates of the channels
